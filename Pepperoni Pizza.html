Pepperoni Pizza

COOK TIME

Ingredients

Pizza Dough

- 1/2 Tbsp active dry yeast

- 2 Tbsp sugar

- 1/2 cups warm water

- 3 Tbsp olive oil

- 1/2 Tbsp salt

- 1 Tbsp white vinegar

- 4 cups bread flour

Toppings (yields enough for 1 13-inch pizza)

- shredded mozzarella cheese (8oz / 200g)

- Tomato sauce

- slices pepperoni

- 1 Tbsp olive oil

- fresh oregano

Making The Dough

In the bowl of an electric stand mixer, combine yeast, 2 tsp sugar and 1/2 cup warm water and whisk until yeast has dissolved. Allow to rest 5 minutes. Add in remaining 1 cup warm water, olive oil, salt and vinegar. Fit mixer with whisk attachment, set on low speed and slowly add in 4 cups of the flour and mix until combined, until dough is smooth and elastic (dough should be slightly sticky but should not stick to a clean fingertip). Cover bowl with plastic wrap and allow to rest in a warm place until doubled, about 30 mins.
