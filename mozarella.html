Mozzarella-Cheese

PREP TIME

COOK TIME

TOTAL TIME

15 mins

5 mins

20mins

Ingredients

- 1L milk

- 7 tablespoon white vinegar

Making The Cheese

1

Pour the milk into a large stockpot,Set the heat in between medium-low and medium.Gently keep stirring the milk so it heats evenly and reaches right around 46C

2

Once the milk gets to that temp,add in the vinegar,mix it well,cover the large stockpot and leave it about 5mins

3

Take a spoon and bring the curd to the side against the pot. Make sure to fish around in the whey, because you will always find extra curd hanging out in there.

4

Stretch and need the mozzarella cheese for a few minutes, then shape it into a ball.Next, immerse the cheese ball into a bowl of cool water for about 10 minutes. Then add some ice to the water and chill for about 5 to 20 more minutes. Some people allow their cheese to sit in cold water in the fridge overnight before eating it.

5

And just like that, the homemade mozzarella cheese is ready.
