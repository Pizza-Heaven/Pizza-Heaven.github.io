New York Style Pizza

PREP TIME

COOK TIME

TOTAL TIME

15 mins

6 mins

21mins

Ingredients

Pizza Dough

- 1/2 cups (274.5 g) bread flour

- 3/4 cup (170.2 g) water

- 1/2 teaspoon dry yeast

- 1 teaspoon salt

- 3/4 teaspoon sugar

- 1 tablespoon olive oil

Toppings (yields enough for 1 13-inch pizza)

- 300 g Homemade pizza dough

- 100 g Mozzarella

- Tomato sauce

- Oregano

- Chili pepper flakes or powder

Making The Dough

1

spread the dough with the exception of the edge with tomato sauce.

2

Cut the mozzarella slices into strips and spread on the pizza.Add plenty of spicy chili powder or chilli flakes. Alternatively, fresh chilies can be used.

3

Bake in the oven preheated to the highest temperature for about 5 to 10 minutes.
